All heavy independent brms fit workloads now have a split-stage contract in `accrual_heavy_fit_stage_registry()`. Worker stages are required to use `accrual_fit_worker_config()` and `accrual_run_task_pool()`. Shared outputs, scores, stacking weights, completed-run pins, reports, and manuscript-facing tables are collector/parent-owned.
